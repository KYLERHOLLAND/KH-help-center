# Downloads & Updates

How to download your Kyler Holland products after checkout, re-download them anytime from your account, and get updated pack files when new versions are available.

---

## Right after checkout

You'll get a confirmation email shortly after you purchase. That's the fastest way to download:

- **From:** KYLER HOLLAND (`store+kylerholland@mail.sellfy.store`)
- **Subject:** Thanks for your purchase! Download your assets now

1. Open the email and click **View your order**.
2. On your order page, click **Download** next to the `.zip` file.
3. **Extract** the archive before installing — see [How KH products install](../getting-started/how-products-install.md).

![Purchase confirmation email|600x100](../assets/purchase-confirmation-email.png)

![Order page with Download buttons|600x100](../assets/order-download-page.png)

### What's in your order

Most orders include two downloads:

| File | What it is |
|------|------------|
| Product `.zip` | The pack you purchased |
| `Product-Support-*.url` | Shortcut to this help center for that product |

First time downloading? See [Getting started — How to download your products](../getting-started/index.md#how-to-download-your-products) for the full walkthrough with screenshots.

## Download again later

Lost the email, got a new computer, or need a fresh copy? Your purchases stay tied to your account — you don't need the original email link every time.

1. Go to **[Sign in](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/)**.
2. Enter the **email address you used at checkout**.
3. Open **Orders** in the sidebar.
4. Find your product and click **View order**.
5. Click **Download** next to the `.zip` file.

![My account — Orders page|600x100](../assets/my-account-orders.png)

!!!tip Wrong email?
You must sign in with the **same email** from your receipt. If you used a different address at checkout, that account holds the order — see [Troubleshooting — I don't see my order](troubleshooting.md#downloads--account).
!!!

**Free products** also go through checkout and appear in **Orders** under the email you used. Re-download them the same way as paid products.

## Product updates

We update packs when Adobe releases major Premiere changes or when we ship improvements to a product. For version-by-version notes — new presets, compatibility folders, breaking changes — see the **[Changelog](changelog.md)**.

!!!tip What's new?
The changelog lists what changed in each release. Re-download first, then check the notes for anything you need to re-import or replace.
!!!

### Get the latest files

When an updated version is available, the **same order download** in your account typically serves the **latest files**. Sign in → **Orders** → **View order** → **Download** again to get the current `.zip`.

You do **not** need to purchase the product a second time for routine pack updates tied to your original order.

### Version folders inside the download

Many transition packs include separate project folders for different Premiere versions, for example:

- **CC 2020 Update 2** — for current Premiere Pro builds
- **CC 2018–2019** — for older installs

After re-downloading, open the folder that matches **your** Premiere version. Details are on each product's help page (for example [Ultimate Effects Pack](../products/the-ultimate-effects-pack/index.md)).

### Replacing an old install

1. **Re-download** the latest `.zip` from your account.
2. **Extract** to a new folder (or replace the old extracted folder entirely).
3. **Re-import** presets or projects using the steps in [How KH products install](../getting-started/how-products-install.md).
4. For project-based packs, keep the full extracted directory intact — especially **Do not delete** or linked asset folders.

!!!warning Don't delete linked folders mid-project
If you're mid-edit with an imported Ultimate Effects or Seamless Flow project, finish or relink before deleting old pack folders. Moving only part of a pack breaks **media offline** links.
!!!

### New computer or clean install

1. [Sign in](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/) on the new machine.
2. Re-download each product you own.
3. Extract and install using the correct method for each file type.
4. Store packs on a drive with enough space — large libraries (sound effects, Ultimate Effects) can be several GB.

## Common issues

### I didn't get the confirmation email

Search your inbox for **Thanks for your purchase! Download your assets now** from `store+kylerholland@mail.sellfy.store`. Check spam or promotions if you don't see it. You can also [sign in to your account](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/) with your purchase email and open **Orders** → **View order**.

### I don't see my order

- Confirm you're signed in with the **same email** from your purchase receipt.
- Check whether you bought the product under a different email (work vs. personal, for example).

More fixes: [Troubleshooting & FAQ](troubleshooting.md).

### Re-downloaded files look wrong or won't open

Reach out at **[assets.kylerholland.com/contact](https://assets.kylerholland.com/contact/)** with your **order email**, **product name**, and **Premiere version** (or other app).
