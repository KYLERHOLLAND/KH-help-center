Due to the nature of digital products, **all sales are final**.

## Compatibility

Please confirm your software setup **before** you buy:

- Use a **recent version of Adobe Premiere Pro** for Premiere-based packs.
- Many assets may work on older versions, but we **cannot guarantee** full compatibility or performance on every build.
- Adobe updates regularly — we work to keep packs current, but older Premiere versions may have limited functionality.

Before checkout:

1. Review the **product description** on [kylerholland.com](https://www.kylerholland.com/products).
2. Watch the **demo or tutorial** on the [Tutorial index](tutorial-index.md) or the product help page.
3. Read the **FAQ** on your product's help page.

LUT and media packs (`.cube`, sound effects, light leaks) often work outside Premiere — check each product page for supported apps.
