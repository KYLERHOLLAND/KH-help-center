# Download Your Purchases

## Right after checkout

You'll get a confirmation email shortly after you purchase. That's the fastest way to download:

- **From:** KYLER HOLLAND (`store+kylerholland@mail.sellfy.store`)
- **Subject:** Thanks for your purchase! Download your assets now

1. Open the email and click **View your order**.
2. On your order page, click **Download** next to the `.zip` file.

![Purchase confirmation email](../assets/purchase-confirmation-email.png)

![Order page with Download buttons](../assets/order-download-page.png)

Your order page includes the product `.zip` and a **Product Support** shortcut that links to help articles for that pack.

## Download again later

If you lost the email or need to re-download:

1. Go to **[Sign in](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/)**.
2. Enter the **email address you used when you purchased**.
3. On the **Orders** page, find your product and click **View order**.
4. Click **Download** on your order page.

![My account — Orders page](../assets/my-account-orders.png)

For the full walkthrough, see [How to download your products](../getting-started/index.md#how-to-download-your-products) in Getting Started.

## Common issues

### I didn't get the confirmation email

Search your inbox for **Thanks for your purchase! Download your assets now** from `store+kylerholland@mail.sellfy.store`. Check spam or promotions if you don't see it. You can also [sign in to your account](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/) with your purchase email and open **Orders** → **View order**.

### I don't see my order

- Confirm you're signed in with the **same email** from your purchase receipt.
- Check whether you bought the product under a different email (work vs. personal, for example).

### Still stuck?

[Contact support](contact.md) and include the email you used at checkout.
