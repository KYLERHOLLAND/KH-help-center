## 2026

### June 6, 2026 — [The Ultimate Effects Pack](../products/the-ultimate-effects-pack/index.md) plugin v1.0.5

**Premiere Pro · [Subscriptions](../subscriptions/index.md)**

The Ultimate Effects Pack **plugin** is now available as a monthly or annual subscription — browse, preview, and insert 700+ transitions and effects without leaving Premiere.

**Plugin v1.0.5**

- **Sign in with email** — use the same address from your [Sellfy checkout](../subscriptions/index.md); no license key to copy
- **Subscription access** — if you cancel, the plugin stays unlocked until the end of your paid period, with a days-remaining notice and **RENEW** button
- **Browse the full pack in-panel** — categories, search, previews, and one-click insert at the playhead
- **Social Versions** — duplicate your active sequence for vertical (9:16), square (1:1), and portrait (4:5) in 1080p or 4K
- **Auto Edit** — place transitions at cut points automatically; filter by category and intensity
- **In-panel update notices** when a newer plugin or pack version is available
- **Help Center** button links to the full [Ultimate Effects Pack guide](../products/the-ultimate-effects-pack/index.md)
- **Premiere Pro 25.6+** supported
- **Panel scale** control for comfortable sizing on any monitor
- **2 device activations** per subscription — see [Sign-in & devices](../subscriptions/sign-in-and-devices.md)

**Effect pack v3.1.0** — updated project zip with new effects. Re-download from your account if you own the pack.

**Get started:** [Install the plugin](../products/the-ultimate-effects-pack/index.md) · [Subscribe on Sellfy](https://kylerholland.sellfy.store/) · [Downloads & updates](../support/downloads-and-updates.md)
