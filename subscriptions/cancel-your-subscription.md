# Cancel your subscription

You can cancel your **Ultimate Effects Pack** monthly or annual subscription at any time. Future charges stop, but **you keep plugin access until the end of your current billing period** — the time you've already paid for.

This is different from the standard Sellfy subscription download flow, where canceling can cut off file access right away. Our **plugin licensing** is built to honor your prepaid period for both monthly and annual plans.

---

## What happens when you cancel

| When | What you get |
|------|----------------|
| **Immediately after canceling** | No more automatic charges. Plugin **still works**. |
| **Until your period ends** | Full access. The plugin shows **Subscription canceled** with **days left** and a **RENEW** button. |
| **After your period ends** | Plugin locks. Resubscribe on Sellfy and sign in again to restore access. |

**Example:** You subscribed monthly on March 9 and cancel on March 20. You are not charged again on April 9, but you can keep using the plugin through **April 9** (the end of the period you already paid for).

The same logic applies to **annual** plans — if you cancel mid-year, access continues until your yearly renewal date.

!!!warning No refunds for unused time
Canceling stops **future** billing. It does not refund the current period you've already paid for — you retain access through that period instead. See [Refunds & purchase policy](../support/refunds-and-policy.md) for general digital product terms.
!!!

## Cancel from your download page

This matches Sellfy's buyer flow for subscription products.

1. Open the **product download page** from your purchase email, or [sign in to your account](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/) → **Orders** → **View order**.
2. Click **Purchase details**.
3. On the purchase details page, click **Cancel my subscription**.

Sellfy will confirm the cancellation. You do **not** need to uninstall the plugin — it will continue working until your paid period ends.

Reference: [Sellfy — Managing subscription purchases](https://docs.sellfy.com/article/208-managing-subscription-purchases)

## Cancel through PayPal

If you paid with **PayPal**, you can also cancel from your PayPal account:

1. Log in to [PayPal](https://www.paypal.com/).
2. Go to **Settings** → **Payments** → **Manage automatic payments** (or **Subscriptions**).
3. Find the Kyler Holland / Sellfy subscription and cancel it.

Canceling in PayPal has the same effect — **no further charges**, and **plugin access through your current period end** on our licensing system.

## After you cancel

### In the plugin

While you're still inside your paid period, you'll see:

- **Subscription canceled** in the sidebar
- **X days left** (or **1 day left** when one day remains)
- A **RENEW** button that opens [kylerholland.sellfy.store](https://kylerholland.sellfy.store/)

When seven or fewer days remain, the notice uses more urgent styling so you don't lose access unexpectedly.

### Changed your mind?

You can **resubscribe anytime** on Sellfy before or after your period ends. If you resubscribe before access lapses, sign-in continues to work as usual. If your period already ended, subscribe again and **sign in** with your purchase email in the plugin.

## Seller-side cancellation

If you need Kyler Holland to cancel on your behalf (for example you can't access your order email), [contact support](../support/contact.md) with your **purchase email** and we'll help from the seller dashboard.

**Note:** Seller cancellation also ends future billing. On our system, you still keep access through the end of your current paid period unless the license is explicitly revoked for abuse.

## Common questions

### Will I lose my project files?

Canceling only affects **plugin licensing**. Any project files or packs you already downloaded stay on your computer. You won't be able to use the **licensed plugin panel** after your period ends without renewing.

### Can I undo a cancellation?

If you canceled through Sellfy or PayPal, start a **new subscription** on [Sellfy](https://kylerholland.sellfy.store/) to continue after your current period ends — or immediately if your access has already lapsed.

### Why does Sellfy say access ends on cancel?

Sellfy's default subscription products deactivate **download links** when you cancel. The Ultimate Effects Pack **plugin** uses separate licensing that keeps your paid period intact. Trust the **days left** counter in the plugin and this help page for access timing.
