Subscriptions

Monthly and annual subscriptions for the Ultimate Effects Pack plugin — sign in, billing, cancellation, and access through your paid period.

The Ultimate Effects Pack plugin for Premiere Pro is available as a monthly or annual subscription. Both plans include full plugin access and the same licensing features — the only difference is how often you're billed.

Plans

Plan Price Billing
Monthly $7.99/month Charged every month on your purchase date
Annual $59/year Charged once per year on your purchase date

Both plans include:

  • Full access to the Ultimate Effects Pack plugin in Premiere Pro
  • 2 device activations per license (see Devices)
  • Automatic renewal until you cancel
  • Pack updates while your subscription is active

Payment is processed through Sellfy using Stripe or PayPal. For PayPal subscriptions, you need a PayPal Business account on the seller side — as a buyer, a standard PayPal account works for checkout.

Your billing cycle is based on the date and time you first subscribed. For example, a monthly plan purchased on the 9th renews on the 9th of each month until you cancel.

How subscriptions work

  1. Subscribe on Sellfy — choose monthly or annual at checkout.
  2. Download the plugin files from your purchase confirmation email or your account.
  3. Install the plugin in Premiere Pro (see The Ultimate Effects Pack for project-file setup).
  4. Sign in inside the plugin with the same email you used at Sellfy checkout — no license key to copy.

The plugin checks your subscription status with our license server about every 12 hours. As long as your subscription is active (or canceled but still inside your paid period — see below), the panel stays unlocked.

Sign in to the plugin

The plugin uses email sign-in instead of a license key.

  1. Open the Ultimate Effects Pack panel in Premiere Pro.
  2. Enter the email address from your Sellfy checkout.
  3. Click Email me a sign-in link.
  4. On this computer, open your inbox and click the sign-in link in the email.
  5. Return to Premiere — the panel unlocks automatically within a few seconds.
Same email as checkout

You must use the exact email from your Sellfy purchase. If sign-in fails, double-check for typos or a work vs. personal address mismatch.

Sign-in links expire after 20 minutes and work once. If the link expires, request a new one from the plugin.

If you cancel your subscription

Important: Canceling does not remove access immediately.

Unlike the default Sellfy subscription experience — where download access can end as soon as you cancel — our licensing system keeps your plugin active through the end of your current billing period. You already paid for that time, and you keep full use until it ends.

Stage What happens
You cancel Sellfy stops future charges. Your plugin stays unlocked until your current period ends.
During the paid period The plugin shows a Subscription canceled notice with days remaining and a RENEW button.
After the period ends The plugin locks until you resubscribe on Sellfy and sign in again.

This applies to both monthly and annual plans the same way.

How to cancel: Cancel your subscription — step-by-step from the download page or PayPal.

Devices

Each subscription license includes 2 device activations.

  • Signing in on a third computer automatically replaces the least recently used device.
  • If you see "This computer is not activated for your license", sign in again with your Sellfy email on that machine.

Switching computers? Sign in on the new machine — you don't need to contact support to "transfer" a slot unless you're locked out.

Renewals and failed payments

When your subscription renews successfully, your access continues without any action on your part.

If a renewal payment fails, Sellfy will retry according to its billing rules. Once the subscription is no longer active and your paid period has ended, the plugin will lock until you renew.

Use the RENEW button in the plugin (or visit Sellfy) to resubscribe.

Frequently asked questions

Can I switch from monthly to annual (or vice versa)?

Cancel your current plan, then subscribe to the other interval on Sellfy. Your access continues through the end of your current paid period after canceling — you won't lose remaining time.

Do I get a license key?

No. Sign in with your Sellfy checkout email and the magic link from your inbox.

What if I change my subscription price on Sellfy?

Sellfy grandfathers existing subscribers — if the seller changes price or interval, new customers get the new terms; existing subscribers keep their original billing until they cancel and resubscribe.

Can I use a coupon on a subscription?

Yes. Sellfy coupon codes can apply to subscription products, and the discount applies to each recurring payment. Routine sales (store-wide discounts) are not available for subscription products on Sellfy.

Is there a lifetime option?

If a lifetime purchase is offered on Sellfy, it uses the same email sign-in flow but does not expire. A lifetime purchase replaces an active subscription license for the same email.

I canceled but the plugin still works — is that normal?

Yes. You keep access until the end of the billing period you already paid for. The plugin will show how many days remain.

Who do I contact for billing questions?

For payment, receipts, or PayPal subscription management, use your Sellfy order page or PayPal account. For plugin sign-in or access issues, contact support with your purchase email.
