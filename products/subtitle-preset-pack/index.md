# Subtitle Preset Pack

Subtitle preset pack with 41+ effects and transitions for Premiere Pro. Elevate captions and lower-thirds with drag-and-drop style presets.

## Installation

### Step 1 — Download and extract

1. Download the product `.zip` from your order email or [your account](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/).
2. Extract the `.zip` file.
3. Locate the `.prfpset` or `.mogrt` files inside.

### Step 2 — Import into Premiere Pro

1. Open the **Effects** panel.
2. Right-click **Presets** and select **Import Presets**.
3. Select **KH SUBTITLE PRESET PACK.prfpset** from the extracted folder.
4. Click **Open**.

A new preset bin appears with 41+ subtitle effects and transitions.

---

## How to use

1. Apply subtitle presets to text layers or captions on your timeline.
2. Drag presets from the imported bin onto selected text clips.
3. Customize styling in the Effect Controls panel as needed.
4. Mix and match effects and transitions for your caption style.
