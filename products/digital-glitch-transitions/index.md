## Installation

1. Download **full** or **demo** `.zip` from the product page.
2. Extract and import the `.prfpset` via **Effects** → right-click **Presets** → **Import Presets**.
3. Expand the **Digital Glitch** bin in Presets.

---

## How to use

1. Place an **adjustment layer** over your cut (same workflow as other KH transition packs) or apply to clips as demonstrated in the video.
2. Drag a glitch preset onto the layer.
3. Preview and tweak in **Effect Controls** — the pack is built for **custom** glitches, not only fixed animations.
4. Swap presets with **Remove Attributes** between tries.

## Tips and workflow

- **Start with the demo** if you're unsure the style fits your channel.
