## Installation

### Step 1 — Download and extract

1. Download the product `.zip` from your order email or [your account](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/).
2. Extract the `.zip` file.
3. Import the audio files into your Premiere Pro project (**File** → **Import**).

### Step 2 — Use in your edit

1. Drag sound files onto your audio tracks.
2. Trim and adjust volume to match your cut.

## How to use

1. Place SFX on your timeline at cut points or key moments.
2. Layer multiple sounds for impact.
3. Adjust volume and add fades as needed.
