## Installation

1. Download and extract the library `.zip` (may be large — use a drive with plenty of space).
2. Keep the **folder structure** intact so categories stay searchable.
3. In Premiere, use **Media Browser** or import `.wav`/`.mp3` files into a **Sound Effects** bin; in a DAW, point the browser to the extracted root folder.

---

## How to use

1. Skim categories (impacts, whooshes, atmospheres, etc.) in Finder/Explorer or inside Premiere's project panel.
2. **Preview** in the project panel or your OS before dropping on the timeline.
3. Place SFX on an **audio track** under video; adjust gain and trim tails.
4. Layer multiple effects (rise + impact) for trailers — watch the tutorial for Kyler's organization tips.

## Tips and workflow

- **Build a favorites bin** in Premiere for the 10–20 sounds you reuse every project.
- **Normalize loud hits** — cinematic impacts are often mastered hot; pull clip gain down before mixing dialogue.
- **Pair with Ultimate Effects** — many [Ultimate Effects](the-ultimate-effects-pack/index.md) transitions ship with sound; this library fills general SFX needs.
