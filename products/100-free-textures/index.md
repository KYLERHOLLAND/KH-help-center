## Installation

### Step 1 — Download and extract

1. Download the product `.zip` from your order email or [your account](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/).
2. Extract the `.zip` file.
3. Locate the `.mogrt` file inside.

### Step 2 — Install in Premiere Pro

1. Open **Adobe Premiere Pro**.
2. Open the **Essential Graphics** panel (**Window** → **Essential Graphics**).
3. Drag **KH 100 TEXTURES** from the extracted folder into the Essential Graphics panel.

The pack appears under **Local Templates** with 100+ customizable animated textures.

---

## How to use

1. In Essential Graphics, drag **KH 100 TEXTURES** onto your timeline.
2. Select the clip and open the **Edit** tab in Essential Graphics.
3. Change settings — image, animation, color, and other controls — to your preference.
4. Duplicate and reuse across your edit as needed.
