## Installation

1. Extract the product `.zip`.
2. **Effects** → right-click **Presets** → **Import Presets** → select the Flicker `.prfpset`.
3. Browse the imported bin (names reflect strobe / exposure / transition styles from the pack).

---

## How to use

1. At a cut or moment that needs impact, add an **adjustment layer** or select the target **clip**.
2. Drag a flicker preset onto the layer.
3. Open **Effect Controls** — adjust opacity, exposure, or built-in keyframes to match your pacing.
4. Stack or swap presets until you get the hit you want; use **Remove Attributes** to reset quickly.

Pair with [RGB Split Transitions](rgb-split-transitions/index.md) when you want color-channel glitches on top of flicker.

---

## Tips and workflow

- **Think modular** — presets are starting points; the tutorial emphasizes keyframing beyond the default.
- **Short hits** — flicker reads best in small windows (a few frames to half a second) unless you're going for a horror sustain.
- **Suggest updates** — the product page/video invite feedback for new flicker styles to add to the pack over time.
