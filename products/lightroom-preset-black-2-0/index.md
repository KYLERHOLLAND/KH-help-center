## Installation

### Mobile (iPhone / Android)

Follow the platform-specific video guides below to import the DNG preset files into Lightroom mobile.

### Desktop

1. Download and extract the preset files from your order.
2. Open **Adobe Lightroom**.
3. Import the presets via **File** → **Import Profiles & Presets** (desktop), or follow the mobile guides if you're on a phone or tablet.
