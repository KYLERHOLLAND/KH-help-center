## Getting started

1. Purchase or subscribe via [Brevidy](https://bit.ly/brevidy-KH).
2. Install the extension and **update to the latest version** (existing subscribers get Brevidy Media by updating — no separate purchase).
3. Open Premiere Pro and launch the **Brevidy** panel from the host application's extensions / window menu (as shown in your install docs).

---

## Animated captions (overview video)

Use this workflow when you need TikTok/Reels-style captions on a sequence.

1. Open your sequence in Premiere Pro and open the **Brevidy** panel.
2. Set **In** and **Out** points on the timeline for the section you want captioned (or caption the full sequence).
3. Click **Captions** to open the caption creation UI.
4. Review **credits** shown at the top of the window — caption generation uses your plan's credits.
5. Generate captions; Brevidy builds styled, animated caption layers on the sequence.
6. Customize style and timing in the panel / on the timeline as needed.

The overview demo captions an entire podcast sequence in a few clicks after setting in/out points.

---

## AutoCut — long-form to social clips (tutorial video)

AutoCut automates pulling standalone moments from long podcasts or streams into vertical-friendly sequences.

1. Create a sequence with your long-form footage (for example a 1+ hour podcast).
2. In the Brevidy panel, choose **AutoCut** (not the basic caption-only flow).
3. Run AutoCut on the sequence — it analyzes the content and generates clip sequences ready for social formatting.
4. Refine cuts, reformat, add stock media, and caption inside Premiere instead of exporting to other tools.

Ideal when you regularly turn YouTube/podcast edits into many short clips.

---

## Brevidy Media — stock inside Premiere (walkthrough video)

Brevidy Media is included with your subscription and works alongside captions.

1. Update Brevidy to the latest version.
2. Open the media browser in the panel to access **videos, images, GIFs, emojis, and sound effects** from multiple stock providers.
3. Search or use **AI recommendations** based on dialogue in your sequence.
4. Insert assets directly on the timeline — no downloading to Desktop, manual import, and resize pass.
5. Apply **intro, outro, and constant animations** to selected clips from the panel instead of hand-keyframing in Premiere.

This replaces the older separate media tool; everything lives in the current Brevidy build.

## Tips and workflow

- **Set in/out before captions** so you only spend credits on the section you need.
- **Update the extension** after purchase to unlock Brevidy Media and the latest AutoCut features.
- **Stay in Premiere** for social pipelines: generate captions → AutoCut clips → drop stock from Brevidy Media on the same timeline.
- **Watch all three videos** — captions, AutoCut, and Media are separate workflows in one product.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Brevidy Media an extra charge?

No. It's included in your Brevidy subscription once you're on the current version.

### Do I still need Seamless Subtitles presets?

Brevidy generates and styles captions inside Premiere. [Seamless Subtitles](seamless-subtitles-v2/index.md) is a separate preset pack for applying transitions to existing Premiere caption layers.

### I lost access or need install help

Use your Brevidy account / purchase email and [contact support](../../support/contact.md) with your order email and Premiere version.
