## Installation

### Step 1 — Download and extract

1. Download the product `.zip` from your order email or [your account](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/).
2. Extract the `.zip` file.
3. Locate the `.prfpset` or `.mogrt` files inside.

### Step 2 — Import into Premiere Pro

**For preset packs (`.prfpset`):** Right-click **Presets** in the Effects panel → **Import Presets** → select the file.

**For motion graphics (`.mogrt`):** Drag the file into the **Essential Graphics** panel.

Apply presets or templates to text layers or captions on your timeline.

---

## How to use

1. Apply text presets to titles or captions, or drag MOGRT templates onto the timeline.
2. Customize text and styling in the Essential Graphics or Effect Controls panel.
3. Trim to match your dialogue or on-screen timing.
