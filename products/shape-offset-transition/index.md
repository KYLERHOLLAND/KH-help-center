## Installation

1. Right-click the `.zip` → **Extract All**.
2. **Effects** → right-click **Presets** → **Import Presets** → select the Shape Offset `.prfpset`.
3. Keep the extracted **shape layer** files in the same folder — don't move only the preset file.

---

## How to use

1. Place an **adjustment layer** (or follow clip placement from the video) at your cut.
2. Drag a **Shape Offset** preset from the bin.
3. If the look references shape media, confirm the shape assets from the download are still linked (same folder as when you imported).
4. Tweak timing by trimming the layer; customize in **Effect Controls**.

---

## Tips and workflow

- **Don't orphan shape files** — moving presets without the shape folder can break the transition.
- **Short hits** — geometric offsets read best at quick cut points.
- **Combine with Crop Slide** for slide + shape hybrid experiments ([Crop Slide Transitions](crop-slide-transitions/index.md)).
