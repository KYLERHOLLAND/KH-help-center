## Installation

1. Download and extract the `.zip`.
2. **Effects** → right-click **Presets** → **Import Presets** → select the cinematic black bars `.prfpset`.
3. Expand the imported bin in **Presets**.

---

## How to use

1. Select your clip (or an adjustment layer above a scene) on the timeline.
2. Drag a **Cinematic Black Bars** preset from the bin onto it.
3. Preview in the Program Monitor — bars should appear instantly without manual cropping math.
4. Swap presets to try different bar thicknesses or aspect feels.
5. To remove, right-click → **Remove Attributes** (or disable the effect in Effect Controls).

Works on **4K drone footage**, 1080p sequences, and other resolutions shown in the tutorial.

---

## Tips and workflow

- **Faster than PNG overlays** — no round trip to Photoshop for standard letterboxing.
- **Adjustment layer option** — place one layer with bars over a whole scene if every clip needs the same ratio.
- **Grade underneath** — apply [LUT packs](10-cinematic-luts/index.md) before or after bars depending on the look you want.
