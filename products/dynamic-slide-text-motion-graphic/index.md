## Installation

1. Extract the `.zip`.
2. **Window** → **Essential Graphics** → drag/install the `.mogrt`.
3. Find **Slide Text** under Local Templates / Browse.

---

## How to use

1. Drag **Slide Text** onto your timeline.
2. In the **Edit** tab:
   - **Intro duration** — e.g. 2s for a slow slide in, 1s for snappy social text
   - **Outro duration** — controls exit speed
   - **Slide direction** — drag the position control so text enters from left, right, etc.
   - **Word vs. line** — slide per word or move the whole line/block together
3. Extend or shorten the graphic clip on the timeline to match dialogue.

---

## Tips and workflow

- **Longer intro = slower reveal** — the tutorial demos 4s vs. 1s intros on the same template.
- **Line mode** for lower thirds, **word mode** for kinetic lyric-style captions.
- **After Effects chapter** — only needed if you're modifying the expression source.
