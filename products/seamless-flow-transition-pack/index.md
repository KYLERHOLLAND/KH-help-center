## Installation

### Step 1 — Download and extract

1. Download the `.zip` from your order email or [your account](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/).
2. Extract the `.zip` to a folder you can find later.

### Step 2 — Import the project into Premiere Pro

1. Open **Adobe Premiere Pro** and your editing project.
2. In the **Project** panel, locate the extracted **Seamless Flow Transition Pack** project file.
3. **Drag the project file** into your Project panel (or use **File** → **Import** and select it).
4. When the import dialog appears, choose **Import entire project** and click **OK**.

The pack imports as a bin of transitions inside your project.

## How to use

### Match your sequence resolution first

Before placing transitions, your **timeline sequence settings** must match the resolution the pack expects (the tutorial uses **1920×1080** as the default).

1. Right-click your sequence in the Project panel → **Sequence Settings**.
2. Confirm width and height (for example **1920×1080**).
3. In the imported pack, use the pack's **Change Resolution** / sequence settings so the transition project matches your timeline. If you edit in **4K** or another size, update the pack sequence the same way — the video walks through resizing for non-1080p workflows.

### Place transitions on your timeline

1. Open the imported transition bin and pick a transition.
2. Drag it into your timeline between clips (follow the structure shown in the tutorial for your edit).
3. Trim and align to your cut as needed.

!!!tip Work at the resolution you deliver
Mismatch between your main sequence and the pack's internal sequence is the most common reason transitions look scaled or misaligned. Fix sequence settings before copying transitions in bulk.
!!!
