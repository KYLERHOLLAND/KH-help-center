## Installation

1. Extract the downloaded `.zip`.
2. Open Premiere Pro → **Window** → **Essential Graphics**.
3. Drag the pack's `.mogrt`(s) into Essential Graphics (or import via the panel's install/browse control).
4. Templates appear under **Local Templates** / your imported folder.

---

## How to use

1. Drag a texture template onto a track **above** your footage.
2. Select the graphic clip and open the **Edit** tab in Essential Graphics.
3. Tune **whites** and **blacks** (and other exposed controls) until the texture blends with your shot.
4. Use as a **full-frame overlay** or trim the clip so the texture only covers a **transition** between scenes.
5. Duplicate the layer and try different textures on the same edit for contrast.

The tutorial also walks through building similar textures in After Effects if you want to understand the underlying setup.

---

## Tips and workflow

- **Blend modes** — try Multiply, Screen, or Overlay on the texture layer if the MOGRT exposes opacity but you need more fusion with footage.
- **Short transition trims** — 6–12 frame texture hits often work better than leaving a texture on for entire scenes.
- **Match contrast before grade** — set whites/blacks before applying a heavy LUT so the texture doesn't clip.
