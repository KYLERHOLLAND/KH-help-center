Custom brush motion graphic MOGRT for Premiere Pro. Create and switch custom brush styles with adjustable color and settings.

## Installation

### Step 1 — Download and extract

1. Download the product `.zip` from your order email or [your account](https://assets.kylerholland.com/u/signin/).
2. Extract the `.zip` file.
3. Locate the `.mogrt` file inside.

### Step 2 — Install in Premiere Pro

1. Open **Adobe Premiere Pro**.
2. Open the **Essential Graphics** panel (**Window** → **Essential Graphics**).
3. Click **Install Motion Graphics Template** (bottom-right of the panel) and select **KH CUSTOM BRUSH.mogrt** — or drag the file directly into Essential Graphics.

The template appears under **Local Templates**.

---

## How to use

1. Drag **KH CUSTOM BRUSH** from Essential Graphics onto your timeline.
2. Select the clip and open the **Edit** tab in Essential Graphics.
3. Customize brush style, color, and other exposed controls.
4. Trim the MOGRT clip to match your edit.
