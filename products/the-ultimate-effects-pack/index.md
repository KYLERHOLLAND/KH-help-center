## Installation

### Step 1 — Download and extract

1. Download the Ultimate Effects Pack `.zip` from your purchase email or account.
2. **Double-click** (or right-click → **Extract All**) to unpack the archive.
3. Keep the entire extracted folder together on your drive — especially the **Do not delete** subfolder.

### Step 2 — Open the pack in Premiere Pro

1. Open **Adobe Premiere Pro** and your editing project.
2. Drag the correct **Premiere Pro project file** from the extracted folder into your **Project** panel.
   - On **Premiere CC 2020 Update 2** and newer, use the project inside the **CC 2020 Update 2** folder.
   - On **CC 2018–2019**, use the matching older project folder instead.
3. When prompted, import the project into your workspace and browse the transition bins inside it.

The tutorial covers placing transitions on your timeline from the imported project structure.

## How to use

1. Browse transition categories inside the imported project (glitches, zooms, spins, etc.).
2. Drag the transition you want into your timeline between or over clips, as shown in the tutorial for that effect type.
3. Trim timing to your cut and preview with audio where the transition includes sound.

Visit the [Ultimate Effects Pack launch site](https://www.kylerholland.com/theultimateeffectspack) to preview categories before you buy or while you explore the pack.
