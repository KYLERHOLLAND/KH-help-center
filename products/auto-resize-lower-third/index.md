## Installation

1. Extract the product `.zip`.
2. Open Premiere Pro → **Window** → **Essential Graphics**.
3. Drag the `.mogrt` into the panel (or install to **Local Templates** per your Premiere version).
4. The template appears under **Browse** / **Local Templates**.

---

## How to use (Premiere)

1. Open **Essential Graphics** → **Browse** and locate the Auto Resize lower third.
2. **Drag** the template onto your timeline above your footage.
3. With the clip selected, use the **Edit** tab to change:
   - **Text** (names, titles, social handles)
   - **Colors** and **fill opacity**
   - Other controls exposed by the MOGRT (swoop in/out timing, etc.)
4. Trim the graphic clip so the lower third enters and exits where you need it.

The MOGRT **auto-resizes** to your text length so you don't manually scale boxes for every name.
