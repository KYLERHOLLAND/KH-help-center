## Installation

1. Extract the `.zip`.
2. **Effects** → **Presets** → **Import Presets** → open the crop slide `.prfpset`.

---

## How to use

1. Park the playhead on the **cut** between clips.
2. Add an **adjustment layer** spanning the transition (or apply per the video).
3. Drag a crop-slide preset onto the layer.
4. Preview; shorten or lengthen the layer to change transition speed.
5. Use **Remove Attributes** to audition other presets on the same layer.
