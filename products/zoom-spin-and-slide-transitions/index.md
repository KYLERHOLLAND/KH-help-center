## Installation

1. Extract the product `.zip`.
2. **Effects** → right-click **Presets** → **Import Presets** → select the pack `.prfpset`.
3. Expand the bin and locate the **1080p** and **4K UHD** subfolders.

---

## How to use

1. Move the playhead to the **center of the cut** between two clips (**Up/Down** arrows jump edit points).
2. From the correct resolution folder, drag a **zoom in**, **zoom out**, **spin**, or **slide** preset onto the outgoing or incoming clip (follow the video for placement on your edit style).
3. Preview — trim clip handles if you need a shorter or longer transition feel.

!!!tip Match resolution folder to your sequence
Using 1080p presets on a 4K timeline (or vice versa) can make motion feel incorrectly scaled. Pick the subfolder that matches your sequence settings.
!!!

## Tips and workflow

- **No adjustment layer required** — faster than layer-based transition workflows for straight cuts.
- **Mix move types** — alternate zooms, spins, and slides down a montage for variety.
- **Compare to Speed Zoom** — [Speed Zoom Transitions](speed-zoom-transitions/index.md) add in-clip zoom ramps; this pack focuses on cut-point motion.
